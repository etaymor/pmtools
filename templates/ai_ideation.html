Frequently Asked Questions

What are AI Ideation Prompts?

AI Ideation Prompts are thought-provoking questions designed to spark innovative thinking about AI integration in your projects. They help teams explore different angles of how AI could enhance, transform, or reimagine solutions to problems.

When should I use these prompts?

Use these prompts during:

  • Design thinking workshops
  • Product brainstorming sessions
  • Team ideation exercises like Crazy Eights
  • Feature planning meetings
  • Innovation sprints
  • AI integration discussions
How do I use these prompts effectively?

For best results:

  • Share the prompt with your team
  • Set a time limit for ideation (e.g., 5-8 minutes)
  • Encourage wild ideas - no judgment during ideation
  • Build on others' ideas
  • Focus on quantity over quality initially
  • Use sketches or diagrams to visualize ideas
How do I facilitate a session using these prompts?

Follow these steps for a productive session:

  • Start with a clear problem statement
  • Generate a new prompt for each round
  • Give everyone time to sketch or write ideas individually
  • Share and discuss ideas as a group
  • Combine and build upon promising concepts
  • Document all ideas for future reference
